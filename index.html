Kimia Performance Analytics

Project Overview

This project analyzes the business performance of Kimia Farma branches across Indonesia between 2020 and 2023. Using transactional and rating data from over 70 cities and 672,000+ records, the analysis focused on identifying sales trends, profit distribution, and top-performing branches. The project was conducted using SQL on Google BigQuery and visualized through Looker Studio.

Objectives

  • To examine branch-level performance based on transactions, ratings, sales, and profit.
  • To identify trends over time in net sales and net profit across multiple provinces.
  • To highlight high-performing regions and branches with consistent profitability and customer satisfaction.

Tools & Technologies

SQL Google BigQuery Looker

Key Findings

  • West Java dominates in both transaction volume and total sales, with several branches leading the national performance.
  • The highest-rated branch was located in Pematangsiantar, North Sumatra, with a 4.8 average branch rating.
  • Over the 4-year span, the company generated Rp346.96B in net sales and Rp98.54B in net profit.
  • Net profit and sales remained stable with only minor fluctuations across months and quarters.
  • The majority of transactions came from 5-star-rated branches, suggesting a link between high customer satisfaction and transaction volume.

Impact

This analysis provides valuable insights for business decision-making by highlighting which branches and regions significantly contribute to Kimia Farma’s overall performance. The correlation between high customer ratings and transaction volume emphasizes the importance of maintaining service quality across branches. Additionally, the stable profit and sales trends over time offer a solid foundation for forecasting and strategic planning. With these insights, stakeholders can better prioritize investments, optimize operations, and develop targeted initiatives to enhance customer satisfaction and regional growth.

Retail Sales Overview

Project Overview

This project analyzes the retail sales performance in the United States from 2015 to 2018 using transactional data from thousands of customer orders. With over 4,900 orders and 5,700 individual sales records, the analysis focused on identifying monthly sales trends, regional performance, customer segmentation, and top-selling products. The project was developed using Power BI Desktop to deliver interactive visual insights that support strategic business decisions.

Objectives

  • To create a dynamic and visually engaging dashboard to monitor US retail sales.
  • To identify top-selling products, peak sales months, and high-performing customer segments.
  • To support data-driven decision-making by providing accessible sales insights to business stakeholders.

Tools & Technologies

Power BI Desktop DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) Microsoft Excel (Data Source)

Key Findings

  • Consumer segment generated the highest revenue, contributing over 50% of total sales.
  • Technology category led in sales, slightly ahead of Furniture and Office Supplies.
  • Canon and Fellowes products were among the top sellers.
  • Sales peaked in November and December, indicating strong seasonal demand.
  • California and New York were among the top-performing states in terms of total sales.

Impact

The dashboard provides valuable insights that empower business stakeholders to make data-driven decisions. By visualizing key metrics such as segment performance, category sales, and regional trends, it enables users to quickly identify growth opportunities and address underperforming areas. The clarity and interactivity of the dashboard help streamline strategic planning across marketing, inventory management, and customer engagement, ultimately contributing to improved operational efficiency and revenue optimization.

E-Commerce Sales Performance Analysis

Project Overview

This project aims to analyze sales data from an e-commerce platform to gain insights into customer behavior, product performance, and overall business health. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of revenue, profit, customer count, sales trends over time, category performance, and payment preferences. These insights help stakeholders make data-driven decisions to optimize marketing, operations, and product strategy.

Objectives

  • To measure total sales, profit, and customer reach over a defined period.
  • To identify high-performing sales months and categories.
  • To highlight patterns and trends for strategic planning and operational improvements.
  • To determine which payment methods and categories contribute above or below average sales.

Tools & Technologies

SQL Python DAX BigQuery Tableau

Key Findings

  • Total sales reached 8.69 billion with a total profit of 2.01 billion, earned from 3,998 unique customers.
  • Sales performance peaked in August and September, indicating effective seasonal or promotional strategies during these months.
  • The Mobiles & Tablets category significantly outperformed all others, followed by Entertainment, Computing, and Appliances.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) and Payaxis emerged as the most frequently used and best-performing payment methods.
  • A notable number of payment methods fell below average in terms of sales contribution, such as productcredit and ublcreditcard.

Impact

The insights gathered from this dashboard allow the business to confidently make data-informed decisions across multiple areas. By understanding peak sales periods, the company can better plan future campaigns to align with high-performing months. The dominance of specific product categories enables targeted product investments and marketing focus, while payment method preferences suggest a continued need for supporting COD alongside the opportunity to grow digital payment adoption. This project ultimately supports strategic planning across sales, marketing, inventory, and customer engagement, enhancing overall operational and financial performance.
